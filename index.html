About Me

Vijay Kumar Koteswar is a Director in Technology Delivery at Epsilon, based in Bengaluru, with over 20+ years of experience leading large-scale technology programs for strategic customers. He brings strong delivery leadership with a deep focus on data, analytics, and AI enablement, helping organizations translate business priorities into scalable technology solutions and measurable outcomes.

In my role, I lead end-to-end technology delivery across complex, multi-stream programs spanning data engineering, cloud migrations, analytics platforms, and AI-enabled solutions. I am accountable for delivery governance, risk management, and driving predictable outcomes across build, integration, and run phases. My recent focus includes leading large-scale data and platform migrations from on-premises environments to cloud ecosystems on AWS and Azure.

I work closely with product, architecture, and engineering teams to ensure delivery plans are technically sound, operationally scalable, and aligned with business commitments, while continuously improving delivery maturity, quality, and speed.

At Epsilon, I have played a key role in strengthening technology delivery capabilities by helping establish the Experience Center, designed to support learning and development through hands-on, platform-led training environments. In parallel, I have supported pre-sales efforts by leading the design and development of multiple demo sites and driving integrations with third-party platforms such as LoyLogic and Medallia.

Prior to Epsilon, I held senior delivery and technology leadership roles at organizations such as LTIMindtree, serving as Technical Manager, Program Manager, Delivery Manager, and Delivery Director. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have pursued leadership development at IIM-Indore, focused on Strategy and Leadership.
